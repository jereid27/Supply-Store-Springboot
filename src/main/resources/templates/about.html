About Us

Meridian Tattoo Supply, nestled in the heart of Meridian, Mississippi, stands as the premier destination
for professional tattoo artists seeking top-quality equipment and supplies. With a legacy spanning
over two decades, we pride ourselves on offering an extensive array of meticulously curated tattoo products, from cutting-edge
machine parts to premium-grade inks and needles. Our commitment to excellence and passion for the art of tattooing drives us to source the finest
materials, ensuring that every artist who partners with us can bring their creative visions to life. At Meridian Tattoo Supply, we
aren't just a supplier; we're your trusted partner in the pursuit of artistic
mastery within the vibrant world of tattooing
